Who we are

R.S. Walsh Landscaping is a family owned and operated, full service landscape design-build company that specializes in custom residential landscape design, installation, and maintenance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to present a unique, personalized landscape design that reflects our client's taste, and exceeds their expectations, providing an invaluable addition to their property. Our devotion to superior client service, from initial concept and design to landscape installation, is the foundation of our company and critical to maintaining lasting relationships with our clients.

Our History

Bob is a visionary person and talented designer. Through hard work and sheer determination, he built a successful, multi-disciplinary company comprised of a landscape architecture and design division, professional landscape installation and custom maintenance division, a tree nursery location and a retail garden center located on Sanibel Island. The company continues to evolve to meet the needs of our clients and supporting the community in which we live and work.

Our space

Currently, we have a design/accounting office located at Jetport Commerce Parkway in Fort Myers where our landscape architects and designers have their workspace. We also have a design office at our Sanibel Island garden center where one of our landscape designers is based. The Sanibel office keeps our designers and landscape architects connected to all of our projects on island and the garden center is designed to be an outdoor showroom for our clients.
